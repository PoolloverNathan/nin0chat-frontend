nin0chat

the chat app of all time. straight to the point, easily accessible, open and lightweight.

how to join

if you're simply taking a look, you can quickly join by entering a username and connecting! if you'd like to control your username, make bots, or gain more permissions, you can also create an account.

rules

they're quite simple: use common sense. filters exist, do not bypass them.
ratelimits also exist, so do not spam. you cannot use guest/user accounts as bots. you have to register them on the dev portal.

join as guest

note: guests are not allowed to send links, attach files or send messages bigger than 300 characters. make an account to lift these limits.
